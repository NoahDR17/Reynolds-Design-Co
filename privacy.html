Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Reynolds Design Co. ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how it's handled.

What we collect

When you submit the enquiry form on this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name (if provided)
  • Any details you include in your message

We do not collect any information automatically through cookies or tracking scripts on this site beyond what is required to load fonts (see "Third-party services" below).

Why we collect it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare your free mockup if requested, and follow up about a potential project. We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to any third party.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers enquiry form submissions to our inbox. Your form submission is transmitted to and briefly stored by Formspree in order to deliver it. See Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.
  • Google Fonts — loads the typefaces used on this site. Loading fonts may involve a request to Google's servers, which can log standard technical data such as IP address, in line with Google's privacy policy.

How long we keep your information

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and, if you become a client, for the duration of our working relationship plus a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. If you'd like your information deleted sooner, contact us using the details below.

Your rights

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have rights under data protection law, including the right to:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete that information
  • Ask us to stop using it, or withdraw any consent you've given

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.
